## Network Participants


ONDC supports various types of Network Participants. Here's a brief overview of each:

1. **Technology Service Provider (TSP) - Buyer**: Provides technology solutions for buyer-side operations in the ONDC network.

2. **Seller NP - Inventory Seller Node (ISN)**: Represents sellers who manage their own inventory and participate directly in the ONDC network.

3. **Seller NP - Marketplace Seller Node (MSN)**: Represents marketplace platforms that aggregate multiple sellers and participate in the ONDC network.

4. **Buyer NP**: Represents buyer-side applications or platforms that enable consumers to make purchases through the ONDC network.

5. **Technology Service Provider (TSP) - Seller**: Provides technology solutions for seller-side operations in the ONDC network.
