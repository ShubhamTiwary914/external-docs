## Getting Started

Follow these steps to get started with the Buyer AdNet Partners platform:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Onboard Your Buyer App">
    Register your Buyer App on the platform to start integrating ads and monetizing your traffic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate Ads">
    Use our easy-to-implement API to seamlessly integrate ads into your Buyer App.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Payments">
    Start earning revenue from user clicks on the ads displayed in your Buyer App.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

- Seamless integration with ONDC Buyer App (Open Network for Digital Commerce)
- Easy-to-use API for ad integration
- Real-time analytics and reporting
- Secure payment processing
