# Onboarding Your Buyer App

<img src="/images/buyers/onboard.png" alt="Onboarding Process" />

The first step in joining our AdNet platform is to onboard your Buyer App. This process requires you to provide essential information about your business and your app.

## Required Information

To successfully onboard your Buyer App, you'll need to provide the following details:

1. **Address Information**
   - City
   - State
   - Country
   - Postal Code

2. **Buyer App Details**
   - Environment (e.g., Production, Staging)
   - Subscriber ID
   - BAP (Buyer App Platform) URL

<Tip>
  We will validate your BAP URL to ensure it's correct and properly configured for ad integration. The URL you provide should be the one where ads will be displayed.
</Tip>

## Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up for an account on our AdNet platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Business Information">
    Fill in your business details, including address information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Buyer App Details">
    Input your Buyer App's technical details, including the environment, subscriber ID, and BAP URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="URL Validation">
    Our system will validate your BAP URL to ensure it's correctly set up for ad integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Submit">
    Double-check all provided information and submit your onboarding request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once your onboarding request is submitted, our team will review the information and approve your Buyer App for integration with our AdNet platform.  <br /> <br />
You'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions on how to proceed with ad integration.

## Next Steps

After successful onboarding, you'll be ready to integrate ads into your Buyer App. Check out our [Ad Integration guide](/buyers/ads) for detailed instructions on how to implement ad displays in your app.
